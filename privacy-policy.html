Frosty Floes Sudoku Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Frosty Floes Sudoku ("we", "our", "us") handles information when you use our app.

No account is required to play. Core game progress is stored locally on your device.

Data Collected

Our app uses Google AdMob and Google consent tools. Through those third-party services, the following data types may be collected:

  • Coarse Location
  • Device ID
  • Product Interaction
  • Advertising Data
  • Crash Data
  • Performance Data

How Data Is Used

  • Third-party advertising
  • Analytics
  • App functionality (for diagnostics such as stability and performance)

Tracking and Consent

We request permission for tracking on supported iOS versions and present consent choices where required by law.

You can change consent choices from the app settings ("Manage Consent") when available.

Local Storage

Your puzzle progress, settings, and in-game state are stored locally on your device. We do not operate a backend account system for this app.
